Natural hot water available here has been a popular attraction for thousands of years.

A winter village site for about 5,000 years, these hot springs had large pit houses typical of plateau communities northwest of here from 4,300 to about 1,200 years ago. After that, small huts used by Great Basin tribes became fashionable. Deer, rabbits and river mussels sustained this Winter camp. After 1842, emigrants using an Oregon Trail alternate also patronized Givens Springs.

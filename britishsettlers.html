Most early Bear Lake settlers came from Britain.

Ann Elizabeth Walmsley Palmer was the first woman convert to the LDS Church in Europe. Born in Preston, England, August 24, 1806, she was baptized July 30, 1837. An invalid, she was carried into the water, but walked out unaided. After coming to Nauvoo, Illinois, in 1842, she drove an ox team to Utah in 1849 and settled there in 1863. She died here November 2, 1890. Through faith she gained the strength to overcome trials and to achieve triumphs.
